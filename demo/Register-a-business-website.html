Register a business website

Last Updated: 06 November 2020

Your domain name (website address or url) can be an important marketing tool for your business. Once you've chosen the name you want, find out how to register it for your business.

What is a domain name?

Your domain name is your website address or internet url. It’s an important marketing tool that can help customers find and identify with your business.

Check if you need to register a domain name

You don’t have to register a domain name when starting or growing your business. But you’ll need to register if you want to set up your own website with a unique domain.

If you don't have your website ready yet, you can still register your domain name and use it when you're ready.

Register your domain name

You can register your domain name through a registrar or reseller listed on the .au Domain Administration Ltd (.auDA) website. Before you register, make sure you have:

  • checked your domain name is available
  • an Australian Company Number (ACN) or Australian Business Number (ABN) if you want a .com.au or .net.au domain

Check if your domain name is available

To find out if your proposed domain name is available, use our Business name check tool. You can also use the tool to check if your business name is available.

Note that a domain name doesn’t give you protection of that name. It simply secures you the url. You should always register your business name first and consider protecting it with a trade mark. If you use a business name or domain name that someone else has already trademarked, you could face legal action.

Find domain options and providers

Visit the .au Domain Administration Ltd (.auDA) website for:

  • a list of domain types (such as .com.au and .net.au)
  • a database of registered domain names
  • links to registrars and resellers, and their prices
Requirements for .com.au or .net.au domains

Different types of domain name have different requirements.

Most businesses use .com.au domains. If you want to buy a .com.au or .net.au web address, you’ll need to:

  • be a commercial entity
  • have either an Australian Company Number (ACN) or Australian Business Number (ABN)
How to choose a good domain

When picking a website domain for your business it's best to go with a name that:

  • represents your business or brand
  • is easy to remember, pronounce and spell
  • is short and unique
